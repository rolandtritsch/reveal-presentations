You can call me a VP Infrastructure Engineering, but my real title is VP of Empowerment and Innovation. Today I am here to talk about a couple of ideas to foster Empowerment and Innovation. One thing is for sure: In a software development organisation, infrastructure done right (the way the infrastructure is build and run and the way the infrastructure organisation interacts with with the rest of the company) has a huge influence on the culture and happiness in the place. It can be a place of fear. Fear that the site is going down when you change something or even when you do not change something, a place with walls and silos and processs and approvals, where change is percieved to be a thread to the stability of the system and therefore needs to be *managed*.

Or imagine a place, where the site is always up and you feel confident about it staying that way regardless what the business is throwing at it. Imaging a place where you feel confident to change the site anytime you want, maybe even multiple times an hour. Imagine a place where you do not have to pick one or the other. Imagine a place where you can have both. This is the place we are building at Gilt. It is a happy place!!! And what you need to make this happen is new thinking and great people and today I am here to talk about some of this thinking and some of the things we have build to support this thinking.
