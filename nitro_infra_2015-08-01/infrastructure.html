... but ... there is (maybe) something missing here.
Imagine that everything is working and that you deploy and experiment a lot. The impact is that you do not need to guess the future anymore (e.g. with traditional Product Management). Instead you can measure your way into the future (guide/guarded by the Head of Product Strategy).
But then the question becomes how to express the Product Strategy and how to make sure that will all of the deployments you are moving towards the better place (or at least do not make it worse).

Infrastructure Engineering

One idea would be to add a feedback platform that is based on an to-be model of the future. That platform uses the metrics gathered by the operations platform (the as-is model) and evaluates, if the deployments actually had a positive impact. This is important, because the volume of the deployments and the complexity involved will make it hard (or impossible) to evaluate the impact of the deployments manually.
